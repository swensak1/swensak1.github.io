Privacy Policy

Angela UGC Posting is a private publishing tool used by Angela to publish her own content to connected social media accounts.

Information we access

When Angela connects a social media account, the app may access basic account information and publishing permissions required to upload or publish content Angela chooses to share.

How information is used

Information is used only to authenticate Angela's connected account and publish content that Angela provides and approves.

Data sharing

The app does not sell personal information. The app does not share connected account information with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

Data storage

Authentication tokens may be stored locally or in the app's private configuration so Angela does not need to reconnect the same account for every publishing action.

Data removal

Angela can revoke the app's access from the connected social platform account settings. Local authentication files can also be deleted from the publishing computer.

Contact

For privacy questions about this app, contact the app owner.
